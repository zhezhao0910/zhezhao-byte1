Question: How's the distribution of fast food restaurants in US?

After analyzing a list of over 10,000 fast food restaurants provided by Datafiniti's Business Database, we figured out that east coast cities like Boston, New York and Washington concentrated much more fast food restaurant s than other cities. In mid-region, Chicago, Cincinnati and their surroundings has high density of fast food restaurants. As for west region, Los Angeles and San Francisco concentrated the most fast food stores.
